About Us

Humankind has never been more open about mental health than today. With this, we have come to realise just how ubiquitous mental disorders are. Over 43 million Americans struggle with mental illness today. It is said that 1 in 5 teenagers has, or will develop, some form of mental illness. These numbers call for drastic action. Our website, Chill-A-Lil, is our answer to this issue. In our own way, we are trying to disseminate a message of hope, a message of “Yes, your feelings are valid, and, most importantly, you aren't alone.”
