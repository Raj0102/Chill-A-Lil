How widespread is this problem?

As of today, 43 million Americans struggle with mental illness. It is said that 1 in 5 between the age of 13-18, has or will develop some form of mental sickness. These numbers call for attention. As a team, this was our call of duty.

What is our mission statement?

Our website, Chill-A-Lil, is our answer to this issue. In our own way, we are trying to disseminate a message of hope, a message of “Yes, your feelings are valid, and, most importantly, you are not alone.” We aim to offer a helping hand in this journey to-wards reaching mental peace. We, as a team, did not see the current situation as an opportunity, but rather as a necessity. We felt that, in some small way, we could give back to society, and that we would grow not just as CS majors but also as individuals. This website is the result of that thought.

What do we aim to do?

We aim to help make those in pain smile a little more. We aim to provide solutions for everything you may be going through, all while keeping in mind the financial constraints you may be subject to. We aim to help rebuild mind, body, and soul.

How do I come to know if I am subject to a Mental Health condition?

Discuss the matter with trusted family members and people close to you. Asking for feedback from those around you can often help determine what you may be going through. Keeping track of fluctuating behaviour patterns is vital with regards to Mental Health. The key is commu-nication, within your own body, as well as with people around you.

Can Mental Health issues lead to addiction?

In desperate attempts to overcome depression, anxiety, extremely negative thoughts as well as disordered eating habits, patients often turn towards substance. These often provide immediate relief, however can have long term implications. It is strongly advised to seek professional ad-vise before self experimenting will pseudo medication.

Can Mental Illness run in the family?

The simple answer is YES. The genetic build up of a person plays a vital role in the devel-opment of a Mental Health condition. If an individual belongs to a family with history of such symptoms, additional care should be taken with guidance from professionals.

Do environmental factors such as noise and pollution increase risk of Mental Illness?

Research shows that there may be a link between the environmental conditions one is exposed to and the state of their Mental Health. Professional treatment can help narrow down what ef-fects certain environments have on potential patients

How effective is treatment with respect to common Mental Health conditions?

It is strongly advised to not keep ones self in isolation, but rather reach out to experts. Between 70-90 % of people who seek proper treatment experience a significant reduction in symptoms.

Can young children suffer from Mental Health problems?

Yes, absolutely. They are often products of biological and psychological factors. These Mental Health problems are often clinically diagnosable. It is necessary to provide children treatment is any such signs surface.

What should I do if I am worried about a friend or relatives Mental state?

It is always best to gently encourage one to seek professional assistance if any such signs surface. Early detection almost always results in early cure, and this holds true in case of most Mental Health conditions.

What are common signs of depression?

Decreased energy, fatigue, weight loss, as well as the act of feeling worthless as well as anxiety are common signs of early stage depression.

What are some ways to improve my Mental wellbeing?

A nutritious and healthy diet filled will fruits and vegetables helps nourish the mind and body. Adequate sleep is also necessary for a giving the mind much needed rest. Also sufficient outdoor activities if possible helps keep the state of the mind in check. Lastly, exercise is often very beneficial to the body as well as the mind. A good balanced lifestyle with correct portions of these can help keep the Mental wellbeing of an individual in check.
